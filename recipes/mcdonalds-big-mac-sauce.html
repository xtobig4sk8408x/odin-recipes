Red Robin's campfire sauce

Description:

Mcdonald's special big mac sauce.

Ingredients:

    Ketchup
    Mayonnaise
    Relish

Steps:

    Add an even amount of ketchup and BBQ sauce into whatever you're going to mix it in (blender, shaker, bowl) and mix until the sauce turns into a nice peach color
    Mix in some relish, don't add too much of the pickle juice.
