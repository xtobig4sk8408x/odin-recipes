Homemade Ranch

Description:

Make homemade ranch using simple ingredients you have at home!

Ingredients:

    Milk
    Mayonnaise
    Fresh Herbs (Parsley, Cilantro, etc.)

Optional ingredients:

    Sour cream/yogurt
    Lemon Juice
    Garlic/Roasted Garlic
    Salt & Pepper
    Chili
    Curry powder

Steps:

    Add an even amount of milk and mayonnaise into whatever you're going to mix it in (blender, shaker, bowl) and mix to get the consistency you want
    Add everything else and mix for flavor
